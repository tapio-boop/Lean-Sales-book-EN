Core Principles

The foundation of Lean Sales

01

Customer Value

Focus relentlessly on what creates value for your customers. Eliminate activities that don't contribute to customer outcomes.

02

Flow Efficiency

Optimize the flow of opportunities through your sales process. Reduce wait times, handoffs, and friction that slow deals down.

03

Systems Thinking

See your sales process as an interconnected system. Optimize the whole rather than sub-optimizing individual parts.

04

Team Collaboration

Build a culture of collaboration where the team succeeds together, not through heroic individual efforts.
